🎿 Skiing

I am an avid skier with a passion for exploring mountains across North America. I have visited over 10 ski resorts and am based primarily at Alta Ski Resort in Utah, where I spend my winters enjoying world-class powder and challenging terrain.

Terrain & Skill Development

  1. Beginner slopes — foundational technique
    • Green runs, groomed terrain
    • Basic turns and stopping
  2. Intermediate runs — varied terrain
    • Blue trails and ungroomed snow
    • Speed and carving skills
  3. Advanced & expert terrain
    • Moguls and steeps
    • Chutes and trees
  4. Backcountry skiing
    • Advanced wilderness exploration
    • Avalanche awareness training

Resorts Visited

  • Alta Ski Resort, Utah
  • Snowbird, Utah
  • Jackson Hole, Wyoming
  • Various other North American resorts

Seasonal Goals

  • Annual powder day tracking
  • Vertical gain milestones
